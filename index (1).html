Philosophy — 01

Luxury is not what is added.
It is what is taken away.

NOIR was founded on a single premise: that the most precious commodity of the modern age is not space, nor service, nor even silence — but attention.

Every corner of every house has been considered by hand. Linens woven in a village outside Kyoto. Ceramics fired by a fourth-generation studio in the Aveyron. Tea sourced from a single mountain in Yunnan.

We do not build hotels. We compose atmospheres, and then we get out of the way.
